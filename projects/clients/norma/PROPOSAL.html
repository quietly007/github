Component Specification Quantity Purpose
IP Cameras 1080p, H.265, PoE, IR night vision, weatherproof (outdoor) 17 Video capture
NVR Storage 8TB HDD (RAID 1 mirror) per location 4 Video storage (30 days)
PoE Switches 8-port Gigabit with PoE+ 5 Camera power & network
Network Bandwidth Min 2 Mbps per camera - Upload to cloud NVR

Storage Calculation:

Per camera: 1080p @ 15 fps, H.265 = ~1.5 Mbps
17 cameras × 1.5 Mbps = 25.5 Mbps total
Daily storage: 25.5 Mbps × 86,400 sec = ~275 GB/day
30-day retention: 275 GB × 30 = 8.25 TB total

Solution: 4x 4TB drives (RAID 1) = 8TB usable per location
Cloud backup: Critical events only
