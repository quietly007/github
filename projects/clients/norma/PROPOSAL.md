Traditional VPN: Star topology (all branches connect to one central point)
**qui3tly.cloud Mesh**: Every location can communicate directly with every other location

### Backup Connectivity (4G/5G)

**Why backup connection?**
- Primary fiber fails → automatic failover to 4G/5G
- Critical systems stay online (POS, security)
- Reduced bandwidth mode (CCTV paused, backups postponed)
- Immediate alert to management

| **Network Bandwidth** | Min 2 Mbps per camera | - | Upload to cloud NVR |

**Storage Calculation:**
```
Per camera: 1080p @ 15 fps, H.265 = ~1.5 Mbps
17 cameras × 1.5 Mbps = 25.5 Mbps total
Daily storage: 25.5 Mbps × 86,400 sec = ~275 GB/day
30-day retention: 275 GB × 30 = 8.25 TB total

Solution: 4x 4TB drives (RAID 1) = 8TB usable per location
Cloud backup: Critical events only
```
