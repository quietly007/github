ARTICLE 1 — PARTIES

1.1 Provider: qui3tly.cloud, operated by qui3tly, Montenegro, reachable at qui3tly@quietly.online (hereinafter: "Provider" or "qui3tly.cloud").

1.2 Client: Montefish d.o.o., a company incorporated under the laws of Montenegro, represented by Dejan Krstović, authorized signatory (hereinafter: "Client" or "Montefish").

1.3 The Provider and the Client are hereinafter jointly referred to as the "Parties".

ARTICLE 2 — DEFINITIONS

2.1 "Services" — all software deployment, configuration, integration, managed hosting, monitoring, maintenance, development and support activities described in Article 3, excluding any hardware procurement.

2.2 "Platform" — the qui3tly.cloud infrastructure cluster (servers Master, Lady, Beauty, Madam) and all associated software, containers, networks and automation scripts.

2.3 "Locations" — the four Client premises: Tivat (HQ), Budva (branch), Ribnjak (production) and Carina (warehouse).

2.4 "Incident" — any unplanned interruption or degradation of a Service, classified by severity as Critical, High, Medium or Low per Article 7.

2.5 "Business Day" — Monday through Friday, 09:00–17:00 CET, excluding Montenegro public holidays.

2.6 "Commencement Date" — the date on which both Parties have signed this Contract and the advance payment has cleared.

ARTICLE 3 — SCOPE OF SERVICES

3.1 The Provider shall deliver and manage the following services for the Client across all four Locations:

3.1.1 Cloud Infrastructure — Setup & Managed Hosting

Deployment, configuration and ongoing managed hosting of the qui3tly.cloud Platform (servers Master, Lady, Beauty, Madam). Includes: VPN mesh network (Headscale/WireGuard) connecting all four Locations and servers; DNS and ad-blocking (Pi-hole/Unbound); reverse proxy and HTTPS (Traefik); security stack (CrowdSec IPS/IDS, Authelia SSO, fail2ban); monitoring and alerting (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki, Uptime Kuma); automated daily backups; container management (Docker/Portainer).

3.1.2 Email Infrastructure

Deployment and management of a full Mailcow email stack (18 containers) for the domain @montefish.me. Includes: Postfix SMTP, Dovecot IMAP/POP3, SOGo webmail, Rspamd spam filtering with AI learning, ClamAV antivirus scanning, TLS 1.3 enforced, SPF/DKIM/DMARC configuration, unlimited mailboxes and aliases, 99.9%+ deliverability guarantee.

3.1.3 CCTV Surveillance System

Software deployment and configuration of Frigate NVR (AI-powered video recording) on the Beauty server, supporting up to 80 IP cameras across all four Locations (Tivat 16, Budva 8, Ribnjak 32, Carina 24). Includes: AI object detection (person, vehicle, animal, package), 24/7 continuous recording, motion-triggered clips, configurable retention (30–180 days), live view dashboard, integration with Home Assistant and Time Evidence. Camera hardware, mounting, cabling and PoE infrastructure are the Client's own procurement and are excluded from this Contract.

3.1.4 Access Control System

Development, deployment and management of access control software for 20+ doors and gates across all four Locations. Includes: RFID card management, PIN code support, biometric integration (fingerprint/facial recognition), real-time door status dashboard, emergency lockdown and evacuate modes, CCTV snapshot on card swipe, audit logs (365-day retention), integration with Time Evidence. Physical readers, electric strikes, wiring and controllers are the Client's own procurement and are excluded from this Contract.

3.1.5 Time Evidence System

Development, deployment and management of an employee attendance tracking system. Includes: mobile app clock-in/out with biometric authentication and GPS geofencing, fixed terminal software (tablet with facial recognition), shift management, overtime calculation, leave request workflows, payroll export (CSV/Excel), daily attendance reports emailed to managers, Montenegro Labour Law compliance, GDPR-compliant data handling. Tablet hardware and biometric terminal units are the Client's own procurement and are excluded from this Contract.

3.1.6 Accounting & Bookkeeping Application

Development, deployment and management of a custom accounting application on the Madam server. Includes: double-entry bookkeeping, accounts receivable/payable, invoice generation (PDF, customisable templates, auto-numbering), bank reconciliation, Montenegro VAT and tax report exports, multi-currency support, expense management with OCR receipt scanning, payroll integration with Time Evidence, financial statements (P&L, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow), role-based access (accountant read-only role), 10-year data archival.

3.1.7 Mobile Applications

Development and publishing of two custom mobile applications for iOS (14+) and Android (10+), built with React Native:
— "Montefish Time" (employee app): clock-in/out, schedule viewing, leave requests, payroll summary, push notifications.
— "Montefish Manager" (supervisor app): live dashboard (all 4 locations), live CCTV view (80 cameras), access control (remote unlock, lockdown), staff attendance, alert management, approval workflows.
Both apps published on Apple App Store and Google Play under the Client's developer accounts or the Provider's enterprise account as agreed.

3.1.8 File Storage & Collaboration

Deployment and management of Nextcloud instance: unlimited file storage, desktop/mobile sync clients, version history, external sharing with expiry and password protection, OnlyOffice document editing, GDPR-compliant.

3.1.9 Training, Documentation & Handover

Staff training (1 hour per group for all employees), manager training (4 hours), accountant training (4 hours), IT administrator training (8 hours); complete user manuals (PDF), admin runbooks, emergency procedures, architecture diagrams; formal handover session with credentials transfer.

3.2 Hardware Exclusion. This Contract covers software, configuration, integration, development and managed services only. The procurement, installation, cabling, maintenance and replacement of all physical hardware (servers, cameras, access control readers, tablets, network equipment, UPS units, etc.) is exclusively the responsibility of the Client and falls outside the scope of this Contract.

ARTICLE 8 — DATA OWNERSHIP & GDPR

8.1 Data Ownership. All data generated, stored or processed under this Contract (including but not limited to employee records, CCTV footage, financial records, access logs) is and shall remain the sole property of the Client at all times.

8.2 Data Processor. The Provider acts exclusively as a data processor on behalf of the Client as data controller, within the meaning of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Montenegro's Law on Personal Data Protection.

8.3 Data Location. All data is stored and processed within Montenegro. No data shall be transferred outside Montenegro without the Client's prior written consent.

8.4 Data Rights. The Client may request a full export of all its data in standard machine-readable formats at any time, at no additional cost.

8.5 Termination. Upon termination of this Contract, the Provider shall deliver all Client data within 30 calendar days in agreed formats. Following confirmed receipt, the Provider shall securely delete all copies from its systems within a further 30 days and provide a written confirmation of deletion.

ARTICLE 9 — SECURITY OBLIGATIONS

9.1 Provider Obligations. The Provider shall: maintain A+++ security certification standard across all managed services; apply security patches within 24 hours of release for critical vulnerabilities; maintain CrowdSec IPS/IDS, Authelia SSO, TLS 1.3 and end-to-end encryption; perform automated daily backups with 30-day retention; conduct monthly disaster recovery drills; and notify the Client of any confirmed data breach within 72 hours.

9.2 Client Obligations. The Client shall: maintain physical security of hardware at all Locations; protect login credentials and not share them with unauthorised persons; promptly report any suspected security incident to the Provider; ensure terminated employees' access is revoked within 24 hours of termination.

ARTICLE 10 — INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

10.1 Custom Development. All custom software developed specifically for the Client under this Contract (including the "Montefish Time" and "Montefish Manager" mobile applications, the Time Evidence system, the Accounting application and the Access Control application) shall, upon full payment, be the intellectual property of the Client.

10.2 Platform Licence. The qui3tly.cloud Platform (infrastructure, configuration, automation scripts and shared tooling) remains the intellectual property of the Provider. The Client receives a non-exclusive licence to use the Platform for the duration of this Contract.

10.3 Open-Source Components. Third-party open-source components (Docker, Traefik, Frigate, Mailcow, Home Assistant, Nextcloud, etc.) are licensed under their respective open-source licences. No additional licence fees apply.

10.4 Portability. On termination, the Provider shall supply the Client with complete configuration files, source code of custom applications and documentation sufficient to operate all systems independently.

ARTICLE 11 — CONFIDENTIALITY

11.1 Both Parties shall keep strictly confidential all non-public information exchanged in connection with this Contract, including technical architecture, business data, pricing and personnel information.

11.2 This obligation survives termination of this Contract for a period of three (3) years.

11.3 Exceptions: information already in the public domain; information independently developed; information required to be disclosed by law or court order (with prior written notice where permitted).

ARTICLE 12 — FORCE MAJEURE

12.1 Neither Party shall be liable for delays or failures caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, armed conflict, government action, nationwide internet infrastructure failure or prolonged power grid outage.

12.2 The affected Party shall notify the other in writing within 48 hours of the force majeure event arising.

12.3 If the force majeure event persists for more than 30 calendar days, either Party may terminate this Contract without penalty by written notice.

ARTICLE 13 — LIABILITY LIMITATION

13.1 The Provider's total aggregate liability under this Contract shall not exceed three (3) times the monthly service fee (€2,250.00 net).

13.2 Neither Party shall be liable to the other for indirect, consequential, incidental or punitive damages, or loss of profits.

13.3 The limitations in 13.1 and 13.2 shall not apply in cases of wilful misconduct, gross negligence, or a confirmed GDPR/data protection violation.

ARTICLE 14 — TERM & TERMINATION

14.1 Initial Term. This Contract is effective from the Commencement Date and has an initial term of six (6) months, corresponding to the advance payment period.

14.2 Renewal. After the initial term, the Contract automatically continues on a month-to-month basis until terminated by either Party.

14.3 Termination by Notice. After the initial term, either Party may terminate by giving 30 days' written notice.

14.4 Termination for Cause. Either Party may terminate immediately if the other Party commits a material breach that remains unremedied for 15 calendar days after written notice of the breach.

14.5 Effect of Termination. On termination: (a) the Provider shall deliver Client data per Article 8.5; (b) any unused portion of a pre-paid advance (beyond the termination month) shall be refunded pro-rata within 30 days; (c) the Provider shall provide portability assistance per Article 10.4 for a period of 30 days.

ARTICLE 15 — GOVERNING LAW & DISPUTE RESOLUTION

15.1 This Contract is governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of Montenegro, in particular the Law on Obligations (Zakon o obligacionim odnosima).

15.2 In the event of a dispute, the Parties shall first attempt to resolve the matter by good-faith negotiation within 15 Business Days of written notice.

15.3 If negotiation fails, the Parties shall submit the dispute to mediation for a period of up to 30 calendar days.

15.4 If mediation fails, the dispute shall be submitted to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent court in Podgorica, Montenegro.

Uključuje: AI detekciju objekata (osoba, vozilo, životinja, paket), kontinuirano snimanje 24/7, klipove pokrenute detekcijom kretanja, podesivu retenciju (30–180 dana), nadzornu tablu sa live pregledom, integraciju sa Home Assistant i Evidencijom radnog vremena.
